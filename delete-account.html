Delete Your Puzzld Data

Puzzld users can delete their account data inside the Puzzld app.

How to delete your data

Open the Puzzld app and go to:

Settings > Delete My Data

This deletes account data associated with your Puzzld account, including saved progress, streak information, username data, and leaderboard data where possible.

If you cannot access the app

If you cannot access the app and want to request deletion of your Puzzld data, please contact Puzzld via Instagram:

puzzld_aus
